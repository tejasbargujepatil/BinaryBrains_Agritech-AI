## üì± Getting Started

### 1. Account Setup
-   Open the app and click **"Register"**.
-   Enter your Name, Mobile Number, and Location.
-   Verify with the OTP sent to your phone (Demo Mode: Use simplified login).

### 2. Home Screen
The **Home Dashboard** is your command center:
-   **Weather Card**: Shows today's temperature, humidity, and rain forecast.
-   **Soil Health**: Displays NPK levels and pH of your soil (based on your location).
-   **Daily Insights**: Smart alerts telling you exactly what to do today (e.g., "Irrigate at 4 PM due to high heat").

---

## üå± Managing Crops

### Adding a Crop
1.  Tap the **+ Add Crop** button on the Home Screen.
2.  Select your crop type (Wheat, Rice, Corn, etc.).
3.  Enter the **Sowing Date**.
4.  Enter the **Area size** (in acres).
5.  Click **Save**.

### Monitoring Growth
-   Tap on any crop card to see its **Growth Stage** (e.g., Vegetative, Flowering).
-   The app will automatically calculate expected harvest dates.

## üí∞ Mandi Marketplace

Check prices before you sell!

4.  **AI Price Analysis**: Look for the green/red indicators to know if the price is rising or falling.
